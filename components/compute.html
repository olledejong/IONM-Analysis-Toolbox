Compute the statistics of Eclipse files

This tool will compute the statistics the user wishes to compute and store these in the configured database (can be changed in the settings). Determining what statistics will be calculated can be done by selecting a possibility from the statistical options. For now these are: 'all', 'p_p_amplitude' and 'auc'. In the future there might be additional statistics that can be computed.
After clicking the run button you will have to fill out an experiment form. Once its filled out correctly, windows containing signals will appear. In these you can deselect signals or change up the ranges of which the statistics will get computed. box.

Please select one or more files you wish to compute the statistics of.
